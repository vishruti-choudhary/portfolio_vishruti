Amazon Sentimental Analysis

This project could be practically used by any company with social media presence to automatically predict customer's sentiment . The process could be done automatically.

The goal of this project is to perform sentiment analysis on Amazon product reviews by employing a Naïve Bayes Classifier. The project involves exploratory data analysis, data visualization, model training, and evaluation, focusing on understanding and implementing key concepts in natural language processing.

In the result we have a trained Naïve Bayes Classifier capable of analyzing sentiment in Amazon product reviews. This model can provide valuable insights into customer opinions, aiding businesses in understanding and responding to consumer feedback effectively.
