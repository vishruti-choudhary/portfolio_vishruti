Road Accident Analytics

The project aims to improve road safety by analyzing a large dataset containing critical information about accidents, such as severity, date, location, and vehicle types. The ultimate goal is to provide actionable insights for traffic engineers and law enforcement agencies.
To address the road accident problem comprehensively, a proposed solution is the implementation of an Integrated Road Safety Management System (IRSMS). This system combines various strategies and technologies to create a holistic approach towards improving road safety.
The dataset is specifically of UK region obtained from Kaggle. It has over half a million records collected over the time period 2019-2022.
